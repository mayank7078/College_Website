Welcome to Dev Bhoomi Group of institutions (DBGI) Dehradun

16+ Years of Excellence

Dev Bhoomi Group of Institutions, Dehradun, Uttarakhand situated in the land of Gods, has stood true to its name and pledged to provide a world-class education and best facilities to its students. With the tenacity to step in the education industry and constant perseverance, today Dev Bhoomi Group of Institutions is amongst the top institutes with multifaceted regimes and courses. Dev Bhoomi Group of Institutions, Dehradun, took its first steps in the world of education as Engineering College In Dehradun (DBIT). Subsequent to its commencement in the year 2005, the growth chart of the Institute has been phenomenal. The institute provides a perfect blend of theoretical and practical knowledge. It is through its perseverance and positive response from the students, that DBIT is now the one of the Best Engineering College in Dehradun, Uttarakhand

Awarded for Best Placement Engineering College in Uttarakhand By MHRD

Dev Bhoomi Group of Institutions, Dehradun, Uttarakhand further spread its wings by introducing the Management courses in the year 2006. Thereafter there was no looking back. Dev Bhoomi Group of Institutions was soaring high and eventually became one of the best management colleges in Dehradun,Uttarakhand.
